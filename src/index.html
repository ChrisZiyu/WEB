Sevens Out

Details

Sevens is a simple dice game that can be played with two or more players. The game is normally played with 6 dices, but it can be played with more or fewer dices depending on the number of the players. To start the game, each player takes turns to roll the dice and determine who will go first. The player who get the highest number will go first. The Goal of the game is to get the total points that the party have decided, each player takes rolls until the Goal is reached. All dices are roll at once. If a player that have less points when rolling loses all the accumulated points.
